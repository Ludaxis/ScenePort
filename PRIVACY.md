# ScenePort Privacy

ScenePort is local-first.

The default bridge binds to `127.0.0.1` and communicates between your MCP client, the local MCP server, and your local Unity Editor. ScenePort does not send Unity project data, source code, scene hierarchy, logs, or asset metadata to a ScenePort cloud service.

MCP clients may have their own data handling behavior. Review the policies and settings of the client you connect to ScenePort.
