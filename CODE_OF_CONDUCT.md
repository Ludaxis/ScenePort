# Code of Conduct

ScenePort follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

Be respectful, assume good intent, and keep technical disagreement focused on the work. Harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, and discriminatory behavior are not welcome in this project.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository owner contact path. Maintainers will review reports in good faith and may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
